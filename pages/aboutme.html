About me

My name is Anders Jensen, and I am a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Liverpool.

Work

My research focuses on the use of proteomic techniques to investigate the effects of ageing, with a particular emphasis on equine research. Alongside this, I have a strong interest in statistics and scientific programming, primarily using R, and I regularly run beginner-level R courses covering a range of topics.

Interests and hobbies

Outside of work I have an unhealthy addiction to running and having my weekends ruined by Stoke City FC.

My website

This webpage was originally a way for me to learn HTML and CSS coding languages. Once I finished coding and designing the page I realised it would be a good place for me to host any projects I have worked on.

Some of the projects are work related and some are more just me messing about with R code. Feel free to contact me about anything related to the website.
